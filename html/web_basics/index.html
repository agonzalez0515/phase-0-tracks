Kevin Owens is WWE Universal Champion

Last night on RAW, Triple H turned on his favorite Seth Rollins to help Prize Fighter Kevin Owens win the new championship

Kevin Owens has now held the following titles:

  1. the NXT Championship
  2. the Intercontinental Championship
  3. the Universal Championship

The Fatal 4-way to determine the winnder included:

  • Seth Rollins
  • Roman Reigns
  • Big Cass
  • Kevin Owens

The big surprise of the night was when Triple H, who has not been seen since April at Wrestlemania, showed up and pedigreed both Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins. The match was a NO DQ so there was nothing anyone could do about it! He handed Kevin Owens the championship!!!
